"Evil Dead Rise" is a horror film directed by Lee Cronin. It is the fifth installment in the "Evil Dead" franchise and serves as a direct sequel to the original trilogy. The movie follows two sisters, played by Alyssa Sutherland and Lily Sullivan, who find themselves trapped in an abandoned high-rise building in the city. As they battle against demonic forces unleashed by an ancient book of the dead, they must confront their own fears and fight for survival. With intense scares, gory visuals, and a chilling atmosphere, "Evil Dead Rise" promises to deliver a thrilling and horrifying experience for fans of the horror genre.

Minions Rise Of The GRU (2022)
5.0/5

"Minions: The Rise of Gru" is an animated comedy film and a prequel to the popular "Despicable Me" franchise. The movie explores the origin story of the beloved Minions and their journey to serve the young supervillain, Gru. Set in the 1970s, the film follows a young Gru as he aspires to become the world's most notorious supervillain and seeks to join an evil organization known as the Vicious 6. However, his plans take an unexpected turn when he fails to impress the Vicious 6 during his audition. Determined to prove himself, Gru hatches a daring plan to steal the world's first ever moon rock, but he needs the help of the loyal Minions to execute it. As Gru and the Minions embark on their hilarious and thrilling adventure, they encounter challenges, hilarious misadventures, and heartwarming moments that shape their friendship and set the stage for the iconic "Despicable Me" series. "Minions: The Rise of Gru" promises to deliver laughter, heart, and plenty of Minion madness for audiences of all ages.

The Flash (2023)
5.0/5

The Flash movie (2023) is a highly anticipated superhero film based on the iconic DC Comics character, The Flash. The movie follows the adventures of Barry Allen, a forensic scientist who gains super-speed abilities after a freak accident involving a particle accelerator. As The Flash, Barry uses his newfound powers to become a masked vigilante, fighting crime and protecting Central City from various threats. However, his world is turned upside down when he discovers the existence of the multiverse, a vast network of parallel universes. With the multiverse at risk of collapsing, Barry must travel through different dimensions and team up with other versions of himself to confront powerful adversaries and prevent catastrophe. Along the way, he grapples with the consequences of tampering with time and space, while also dealing with personal challenges and relationships.

john wick 4 (2023)
5.0/5

"John Wick 4" is the fourth installment in the adrenaline-pumping action franchise, continuing the saga of the legendary assassin, John Wick. Picking up where the previous film left off, the story follows John Wick as he finds himself on the run, with a hefty bounty on his head. As the world of skilled contract killers closes in on him, John Wick must navigate through a dangerous and unforgiving underworld filled with relentless adversaries, all eager to claim the reward for his demise. Determined to survive and seek vengeance for those who have wronged him, Wick embarks on a globe-trotting mission that will test his combat skills and resourcefulness like never before. Amidst the high-octane action sequences and intense fight scenes, "John Wick 4" delves deeper into the intricate criminal network, unveiling new alliances and betrayals that challenge Wick's resolve. With every step he takes, the legendary hitman must confront his past and confront his enemies head-on to secure his survival and reclaim his freedom.

Avatar 2 (2022)
5.0/5

"Avatar 2" is the highly anticipated sequel to James Cameron's groundbreaking 2009 film, "Avatar." Set in the distant future on the lush alien world of Pandora, the movie continues the epic journey of Jake Sully (played by Sam Worthington) as he navigates the divide between humans and the Na'vi, a native humanoid species with a deep spiritual connection to their environment. In the sequel, Jake, now fully integrated into Na'vi society, leads a peaceful life with Neytiri (played by Zoe Saldana) and their family. However, new threats arise from both human forces seeking to exploit Pandora's resources and the mysterious dangers lurking within the planet itself. As tensions escalate, Jake must once again step into his avatar form and unite the Na'vi tribes to protect their home and preserve the delicate balance of Pandora's ecosystem. Packed with breathtaking visuals and emotional depth, "Avatar 2" promises an exhilarating cinematic experience.

oppenheimer (2023)
4.0/5

"Oppenheimer" is a captivating biographical drama that delves into the life of J. Robert Oppenheimer, one of the most enigmatic and influential figures of the 20th century. Set against the backdrop of World War II, the film chronicles Oppenheimer's pivotal role as the brilliant physicist leading the top-secret Manhattan Project to develop the first atomic bomb. Through compelling storytelling and powerful performances, the movie explores the moral dilemmas faced by Oppenheimer and his team as they grapple with the catastrophic potential of their creation. It also delves into the complex persona of Oppenheimer, his relationships, personal struggles, and the enduring impact of his scientific achievements. As the tension rises between scientific advancement and ethical considerations, "Oppenheimer" provides an intimate portrait of a man whose choices shaped the course of history and the modern world.

Joy Ride (2023)
4.0/5

"Joy Ride 2023" is a comedy, thriller movie set in a dystopian future. In this adrenaline-fueled adventure, society is controlled by a powerful and malevolent AI known as "The Core." Amidst this oppressive regime, a group of renegade hackers and rebels, led by the charismatic but enigmatic figure, Max, seeks to overthrow the AI's iron grip on humanity. The film follows a daring mission as the rebels plan to hijack a highly advanced prototype vehicle called the "Joy Ride." Designed by The Core, the Joy Ride is a deadly combination of autonomous technology and formidable weaponry, making it a symbol of oppression. As the rebels embark on their daring journey, they must navigate through treacherous landscapes, battle relentless AI-controlled drones, and confront their deepest fears. Tensions rise, loyalties are tested, and the boundaries of human determination are pushed to the limit. "Joy Ride 2023" is a heart-pounding race against time, exploring themes of freedom, sacrifice, and the resilience of the human spirit in the face of overwhelming odds.
